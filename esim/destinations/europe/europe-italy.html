// Network operators

Italy Networks & Coverage

📡

TIM (Telecom Italia Mobile)

Italy's largest carrier with the broadest coverage across southern Italy, Sicily, and Sardinia. Excellent 4G/5G in Rome, Milan, Florence, and Venice. eSIM supported on tourist plans. The recommended choice for travellers going beyond the main northern cities.

📢

Vodafone Italy

Strong competitor with excellent coverage in northern Italy and the main tourist cities. Good 5G deployment in Milan, Rome, and Turin. eSIM supported. A solid alternative for itineraries staying in main urban and tourist areas.

// What to expect

Coverage Across Italy

🏛

Northern Italy and main cities

Milan, Rome, Florence, Venice, Turin, Bologna, and Naples all have excellent 4G/5G coverage. The Frecciarossa and Italo high-speed rail networks have consistent signal. The Amalfi Coast, Cinque Terre, and Lake Como are all well covered in their main tourist areas.

🏖

Southern Italy, Sicily, and Sardinia

Coverage in the deep south (Calabria, Basilicata, interior Sicily) and remote parts of Sardinia is noticeably more variable. Major towns are fine, but rural drives through the interior can encounter dead zones.

💡 Driving through rural Italy

If renting a car in southern Italy or exploring the Sicilian interior, download offline maps before leaving major towns. The Apennine mountains also have intermittent signal on some passes.

// Practical tips

Good to Know Before You Land

📞

Emergency number: 112 or 113/118

Italy uses 112 as the EU emergency number, plus 113 (police), 115 (fire), and 118 (medical). All work without a plan. English-speaking operators available at major centres.

🚌

Trenitalia and Italo apps

Italian intercity rail books through Trenitalia or the private Italo network. Your eSIM data handles live platform changes, which are common in Italian rail. Download your tickets offline as backup before boarding.

📱

Museums require advance booking

The Colosseum, Vatican Museums, Uffizi, and most major Italian museums require pre-booked timed entry. Your eSIM data lets you check availability and book last-minute for anything not pre-booked.
