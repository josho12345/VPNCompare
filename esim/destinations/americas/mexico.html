// Network operators

Mexico Networks & Coverage

📡

Telcel

Mexico's largest carrier by far with the broadest coverage nationwide including rural areas. Excellent 4G in Mexico City, Cancun, Guadalajara, and all main tourist destinations. eSIM supported. The only serious choice for travel beyond the main resort areas.

📢

AT&T Mexico

Strong competitor with excellent coverage in major cities and tourist corridors. Good 4G in Mexico City and the Yucatan Peninsula. eSIM supported. A reliable alternative for travellers sticking to main tourist destinations.

// What to expect

Coverage Across Mexico

🏛

Cities, resorts, and tourist areas

Mexico City, Cancun, Playa del Carmen, Tulum, Guadalajara, Monterrey, and Oaxaca city all have reliable 4G coverage. The main tourist highway corridors are well covered. Cancun airport and the Riviera Maya strip are excellent.

🏔

Rural Mexico and remote areas

The Copper Canyon (Barranca del Cobre), rural Chiapas, the Oaxacan highlands, parts of the Yucatan jungle, and remote indigenous communities can have very limited or no coverage. The Chihuahua al Pacifico railway through the Copper Canyon is largely offline.

💡 Safety and staying connected

Download offline Google Maps for your entire Mexico route before leaving any city. In areas with limited signal, your eSIM is your lifeline for navigation and emergency services. Keep data active and check local safety advisories via your government travel app before heading to any new region.

// Practical tips

Good to Know Before You Land

📞

Emergency number: 911

Mexico adopted the unified 911 emergency number in 2016. Works without a plan. Tourist police (SECTUR) in resort areas: 078. English-speaking operators available in major tourist areas. In remote areas, response times can be significantly longer.

🚌

ADO buses and domestic flights

ADO is Mexico's premium long-distance bus network — reliable, comfortable, and air-conditioned. Your eSIM data handles ADO booking and domestic flight check-in on Aeromexico or VivaAerobus. Download tickets offline before travelling to areas with limited signal.

📱

WhatsApp is essential in Mexico

WhatsApp is the primary communication tool for everything in Mexico — booking restaurants, contacting hotels, arranging tours. Keep your eSIM data active and give your WhatsApp number to every accommodation and tour operator you deal with.
