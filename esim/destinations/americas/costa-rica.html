// Network operators

Costa Rica Networks & Coverage

📡

Kolbi (ICE)

Costa Rica's national carrier with the broadest coverage including remote areas. Good 4G in San Jose, La Fortuna, Manuel Antonio, and Tamarindo. eSIM supported. The recommended choice for travel into national parks and remote areas.

📢

Movistar Costa Rica

Strong competitor with excellent coverage in San Jose and main tourist corridors. Good 4G in the Central Valley and Pacific coast resorts. eSIM supported. A solid alternative for travellers sticking to main tourist destinations.

// What to expect

Coverage Across Costa Rica

🏛

San Jose, tourist areas, and Pacific coast

San Jose, La Fortuna (Arenal), Manuel Antonio, Tamarindo, Jaco, Monteverde, and Liberia all have reliable 4G coverage. The main Pacific coast tourist corridor from Liberia to Manuel Antonio is well covered.

🌳

Remote rainforest and Caribbean coast

The Osa Peninsula, Corcovado National Park, remote Caribbean coast near Tortuguero, and parts of La Amistad International Park have limited or no coverage. Tortuguero is only accessible by boat or plane and has very limited connectivity.

💡 Wildlife and national park preparation

Costa Rica's national parks are the main attraction — but many have limited or no mobile signal inside them. Download offline maps, save your lodge or hotel contact details, and confirm your guide's number before entering any national park. For Corcovado, you must go with a licensed guide — they are your communication link in emergencies.

// Practical tips

Good to Know Before You Land

📞

Emergency number: 911

Costa Rica uses 911 for all emergencies. Works without a plan. English-speaking operators available. Red Cross ambulance: 128. Coast guard: 1800-800-8000. For marine emergencies on both coasts keep the coast guard number saved.

🚌

Domestic shuttles and small aircraft

Shared shuttles connect all main tourist destinations. Small aircraft (Nature Air, Sansa) provide fast connections to remote areas like the Osa Peninsula. Your eSIM data handles shuttle booking through Interbus or GoTocostarica. Download tickets offline before travelling to areas with limited signal.

📱

WhatsApp runs Costa Rica

WhatsApp is how everything works in Costa Rica — tour booking, shuttle reservations, hotel check-in, restaurant tables. Keep your eSIM data active from arrival and share your WhatsApp number with every operator you deal with.
