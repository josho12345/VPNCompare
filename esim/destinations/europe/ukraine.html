// Network operators

Ukraine Networks & Coverage

📡

Kyivstar

Ukraine's largest carrier and the one with the most resilient infrastructure in western Ukraine. Kyivstar has worked to maintain network operations despite infrastructure challenges. Coverage in Lviv and western cities is reasonable.

📢

Vodafone Ukraine

Vodafone Ukraine operates in accessible parts of the country with reasonable urban coverage in western Ukraine. Like all Ukrainian operators, network capacity has been significantly impacted by the ongoing conflict.

// What to expect

Coverage in Ukraine — The Honest Picture

🏛

Western Ukraine and Lviv

Lviv, Ivano-Frankivsk, Ternopil, and western Ukrainian cities have the most stable mobile coverage in the country. Lviv in particular has maintained relatively normal urban life and connectivity.

⚠

Kyiv and eastern regions

Kyiv has partial and variable mobile coverage affected by infrastructure damage. Eastern oblasts — Donetsk, Luhansk, Zaporizhzhia, Kherson — have severely disrupted or non-functional mobile infrastructure. Do not travel to these regions.

💡 Travel advisory — please read carefully

The UK FCDO, US State Department, and most Western governments advise against all travel to Ukraine or all but essential travel to specific regions. If travelling to Ukraine for legitimate purposes, work with experienced local contacts. Satellite communicators are strongly recommended over relying on mobile signal.

// Practical tips

Good to Know Before You Land

📞

Emergency number: 112

Ukraine uses 112 as the main emergency number. Works without a plan where network infrastructure is operational. Air raid warnings are distributed via the Povitryani Syly app — keep this installed and active if in Ukraine.

🚌

Ukrzaliznytsia rail and ground transport

Ukrainian rail (Ukrzaliznytsia) has continued operating western routes throughout the conflict. Overnight trains from Lviv to Kyiv and Kyiv to Przemysl (Poland border) have been widely used. Check current schedule and safety conditions before booking.

⚛

Register with your government before travel

If travelling to Ukraine, register with your country's embassy or travel registration service before entry. Your eSIM data lets you register and check advisories in real time. Carry physical backup documentation at all times.
