// Network operators

Dominican Republic Networks & Coverage

📡

Claro Dominican Republic

The largest carrier with the broadest coverage including the interior provinces. Excellent 4G in Punta Cana, Santo Domingo, and Puerto Plata. eSIM supported. The recommended choice for travel beyond the main resort corridor.

📢

Altice Dominican Republic

Strong competitor with excellent coverage in Santo Domingo and main tourist areas. Good 4G in Punta Cana and the north coast. eSIM supported. A solid alternative for travellers sticking to the main resort destinations.

// What to expect

Coverage Across the Dominican Republic

🏛

Resort areas and main cities

Punta Cana, Santo Domingo, Puerto Plata, La Romana, Samana (main town), and all major resort areas have reliable 4G coverage. The main highway network between cities is well covered. Both airports have excellent connectivity.

🏔

Interior and remote areas

The interior Cordillera Central mountains, remote beaches on the peninsula coasts, and the border areas with Haiti can have more variable coverage. Jarabacoa and Constanza in the mountains have reasonable signal in town but limited coverage on hiking trails.

💡 Resort versus independent travel

The Dominican Republic divides clearly between all-inclusive resort travel (where coverage is excellent and you rarely leave the resort) and independent travel (where coverage varies more). If venturing beyond your resort, download offline maps of your planned areas and save your accommodation and transport contacts before leaving.

// Practical tips

Good to Know Before You Land

📞

Emergency number: 911

The Dominican Republic uses 911 for all emergencies. Works without a plan. Tourist police (POLITUR) operate in major resort areas and respond to tourist incidents. English-speaking operators available in main tourist areas.

🚌

Getting around the Dominican Republic

Guaguas (shared minibuses) are cheap but crowded. Uber operates in Santo Domingo and Punta Cana. Caribe Tours and Metro buses are the better intercity bus options. Your eSIM data handles Uber booking and navigation between destinations.

📱

All-inclusive WiFi versus eSIM

Most all-inclusive resorts offer free WiFi — but it is often slow and congested. Your eSIM gives you reliable data outside the resort for day trips, excursions, restaurant bookings in town, and navigating Santo Domingo without relying on resort-provided transport.
