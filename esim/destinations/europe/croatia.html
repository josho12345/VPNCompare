// Network operators

Croatia Networks & Coverage

📡

Hrvatski Telekom (HT)

Croatia's largest carrier with the broadest coverage across the islands and inland areas. Excellent 4G in Dubrovnik, Split, Zadar, and Rijeka. eSIM supported. The recommended choice for island hopping.

📢

A1 Croatia

Strong competitor with excellent coverage along the Adriatic coast and in main tourist areas. Good 4G in all major cities. eSIM supported. A solid alternative for travellers sticking to main coastal resorts.

// What to expect

Coverage Across Croatia

🏛

Coastal cities and resort towns

Dubrovnik, Split, Zadar, Rijeka, Rovinj, Pula, and the main tourist resort areas along the Adriatic coast all have reliable 4G coverage. The main Adriatic Highway has consistent signal. Major ferry terminals are generally well covered.

🌊

Islands and inland areas

Croatia has 1,244 islands of which 78 are inhabited. Coverage on the main tourist islands is reasonable in their main towns but thinner in coves. More remote islands like Vis and Lastovo have very limited coverage. Inland Slavonia is more variable.

💡 Island ferry crossings

Ferry crossings between Croatian islands can be entirely without signal. Download offline maps of every island you plan to visit before boarding your first ferry. Accommodation addresses and boat operator contacts should be saved offline.

// Practical tips

Good to Know Before You Land

📞

Emergency number: 112

Croatia uses 112 as the EU emergency number. Specialist numbers: 192 (police), 193 (fire), 194 (ambulance), 195 (sea rescue). Sea rescue is particularly relevant for sailors and kayakers on the Adriatic.

⛴

Jadrolinija ferries and booking

Jadrolinija is the main Croatian ferry operator. Book tickets in advance in July and August — car ferries on popular routes sell out. The Jadrolinija app handles booking with your eSIM active.

📱

Dubrovnik and Plitvice require advance booking

Dubrovnik city walls and Plitvice Lakes require pre-booked entry during peak summer. Book online before arrival. Your eSIM data handles booking and real-time crowd information for the most popular sites.
