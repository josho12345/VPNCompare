// Our Methodology

How We Test VPNs

Every VPN on this site is put through a rigorous 8-point testing framework by our independent editorial team. We never accept payment to influence rankings.

⚡

Speed Testing

We run 50+ speed tests across UK, US, EU, and Asia-Pacific servers using a 1Gbps test connection. Results are averaged over 30 days.

🔒

Privacy Audit

We verify no-log claims by reviewing third-party audits from firms including Deloitte, PwC, and Cure53. We check for DNS, WebRTC, and IPv6 leaks.

🎬

Streaming Tests

Each VPN is tested against Netflix UK/US, BBC iPlayer, Disney+, Amazon Prime, and HBO Max from 5 different countries.

🛡

Security Analysis

We examine encryption standards (AES-256), protocol quality (WireGuard, OpenVPN), kill switch reliability, and jurisdiction of incorporation.

💼

Business Features

For business VPNs, we evaluate team management dashboards, SSO/SAML support, dedicated IPs, compliance tools, and SLA guarantees.

💰

Value Assessment

We compare price per device, plan flexibility, refund policies, and whether the feature-set justifies the cost at each tier.
