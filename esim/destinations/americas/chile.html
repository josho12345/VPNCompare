// Network operators

Chile Networks & Coverage

📡

Entel

Chile's largest carrier with the broadest coverage across Chile's 4,300km length. Excellent 4G in Santiago, Valparaiso, and all main cities. Best coverage in Patagonia towns. eSIM supported.

📢

Movistar Chile

Strong competitor with excellent urban coverage in Santiago and northern Chilean cities. Good 4G in the Atacama region. eSIM supported. A solid alternative for travellers focused on Santiago and northern Chile.

// What to expect

Coverage Across Chile

🏛

Santiago and northern Chile

Santiago, Valparaiso, Vina del Mar, Antofagasta, and San Pedro de Atacama all have excellent 4G coverage. The Atacama Desert around San Pedro has better coverage than most travellers expect — the main tourist areas and the road to the salt flats are covered.

🏔

Patagonia and the far south

Puerto Natales and Punta Arenas have coverage in their town centres. The Torres del Paine national park trails have no coverage. The Carretera Austral (Southern Highway) has coverage in main towns but extensive gaps between them. Download everything before leaving any town.

💡 Driving the Carretera Austral

The Carretera Austral is one of the world's great road trips — 1,200km of unpaved road through Chilean Patagonia. Download offline maps for the entire route before leaving Puerto Montt. Save every accommodation address, ferry crossing time, and emergency contact offline. Signal disappears for hours at a time on this route.

// Practical tips

Good to Know Before You Land

📞

Emergency number: 131 / 132 / 133

Chile emergency numbers: ambulance 131, fire 132, police 133. CONAF (national parks) rangers coordinate park emergencies. In Patagonia, CONAF rangers at park entrances are your first emergency contact on trail.

🚌

LATAM domestic flights and Navimag ferry

LATAM Chile connects Santiago with Puerto Montt, Punta Arenas, and other cities. The Navimag ferry from Puerto Montt to Puerto Natales is a 4-day journey through the fjords with very limited connectivity. Download everything before boarding.

🍲

Chilean wine regions

The Maipo, Colchagua, and Casablanca wine valleys around Santiago all have good mobile coverage. Most wineries accept bookings via their websites or WhatsApp. Your eSIM data handles navigation between vineyards and restaurant bookings in the wine regions.
