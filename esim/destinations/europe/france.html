// Network operators

France Networks & Coverage

📡

Orange France

France's largest and most extensive carrier. Best rural coverage across the country and the widest network footprint outside major cities. Excellent 4G/5G in Paris, Lyon, Marseille, and all major cities. eSIM supported.

📢

SFR

Strong competitor with excellent urban coverage in Paris and major French cities. 5G expanding rapidly in city centres. eSIM supported. A solid alternative for travellers keeping to cities and main tourist destinations.

// What to expect

Coverage Across France

🏛

Paris, cities, and tourist hotspots

Paris has outstanding 4G/5G coverage across all arrondissements including the Metro, tourist attractions, and both airports. The Riviera from Nice to Cannes is excellent. Lyon, Bordeaux, Strasbourg, and Marseille are all well covered.

🌳

Rural France and mountain areas

The Massif Central, parts of rural Brittany, the Pyrenees, and remote Provence villages can have limited or no coverage. France is the largest country in Western Europe and much of its interior is genuinely rural.

💡 Driving through rural France

Many of France's most picturesque regions — the Dordogne, the Lot valley, Correze, parts of Burgundy — have intermittent coverage. Download offline Google Maps before leaving any major city if driving rural routes.

// Practical tips

Good to Know Before You Land

📞

Emergency number: 112 or 15/17/18

France uses the EU standard 112 for all emergencies, plus SAMU medical is 15, police is 17, fire is 18. All work without a plan. English-speaking operators are generally available.

🚌

Paris transit: the RATP app

The Paris Metro, RER, buses, and Velib bike-share all integrate with the Bonjour RATP app. You need active data to use it. Your eSIM provides this from the moment you land at CDG or Orly.

📱

SNCF Connect for intercity trains

Intercity French trains via TGV book through SNCF Connect. Tickets are significantly cheaper booked in advance. Your eSIM data lets you check times, book, and display tickets from your phone.
